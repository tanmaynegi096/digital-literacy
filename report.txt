This project focuses on digital literacy and its importance in today’s world. Digital literacy refers to the ability to use digital tools, platforms, and technologies safely and effectively.

In Task 1, I created an infographic highlighting important cybersecurity tips such as using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and avoiding suspicious links.

In Task 2, I developed a digital portfolio by creating profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand how to build an online presence.

In Task 3, I explored coding platforms and completed a basic problem on HackerRank, which improved my problem-solving skills.

In Task 4, I learned about email etiquette and created sample emails along with a checklist for proper communication.

In Task 5, I studied cybercrime, specifically phishing attacks, and created a case study along with a prevention checklist.

Overall, this project helped me understand how to stay safe online, use digital tools responsibly, and improve my digital skills.
